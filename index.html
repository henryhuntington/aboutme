Table Talk

This is a type of table!

I recently built this table to go next to the stove to increase countertop space in our kitchen. It enables many cooking tasks. Since I took this picture, I put it where it goes and put hooks under the top surface to hang pots and pans. Soon, I will take it out and sand it down some more and maybe treat it with polyurethane. We'll see if I have time now that I'm doing coding bootcamp.

Tables are really fun to build! Here's how:

  1. 1. Make a sketch that includes the dimensions of the table.
  2. 2. Source the materials. Reclaimed wood is thrifty and can be good looking.
  3. 3. Make appropriate cuts to fit your dimensions.
  4. 4. Assemble the parts without using fasteners (a dry fit) to check for a good measurements.
  5. 5. If everything is cut properly, glue and fasten the parts together.
  6. 6. Enjoy your spectacular, functional table and bask in other's appreciation of it.
  7. 7. Go to bed already.
